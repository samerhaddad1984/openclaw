## GENERAL RULES FOR ALL EMAILS

1. **Keep it short.** Under 150 words for cold outreach. Under 200 for follow-ups.
2. **One CTA per email.** Don't ask them to watch a video AND book a demo AND visit your website.
3. **Personalize everything in brackets.** Generic emails get deleted.
4. **Send between 8:00 and 10:00 AM on Tuesday-Thursday.** Highest open rates.
5. **Never use "Dear" or "To whom it may concern."** Use their first name.
6. **Never attach files in cold emails.** They trigger spam filters. Link instead.
7. **Use "Re:" in follow-up subjects** to create thread continuity.
8. **Include your phone number.** Some people prefer to call.
